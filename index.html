About

My work approaches design as a way to organize complex information, shape meaningful environments, and make ideas visible through disciplined visual structure. I value clarity, evidence, and thoughtful iteration.

This homepage is designed as a concise academic introduction for professors and reviewers. The full portfolio PDF provides detailed project process, visual documentation, and final outcomes.

02

Research and Design Interests

Research-led design practice

Using observation, precedent study, and structured analysis to define design problems before form-making.

Spatial and experiential narratives

Exploring how people move through, interpret, and remember designed environments and exhibitions.

Visual communication systems

Building clear hierarchies, identities, diagrams, and presentation systems for complex project materials.

Digital interface and information design

Designing structured digital experiences that support scanning, comparison, and decision-making.
